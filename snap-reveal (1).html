Concrete Change 03 — Safety & Report Inverted

Report a Safety Concern.

If you ever experience harassment, bullying or any other safety concern, you can always report it straight to us.

What happens to my report?

Your report is reviewed by our Trust & Safety team within 24 hours. We take all reports seriously and investigate each one according to our Community Guidelines.

Will my report be kept confidential?

We keep your report details private. The reported user will not know who filed the report.

Can I delete my account?

You can deactivate your account at any time. Deactivation is the first step to deletion.

Delete Account

Once deleted, your account and data will be removed.
